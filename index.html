Sovereign Renovations

Let's partner on your next home project.

We can work with you to design your dream kitchen! We're ready to help you reimagine everything from flooring to lighting, tiling to countertops, cabinets to wall paint, and much more! As we work to bring design, colors, patterns, and finishes to your kitchen, you'll see how an updated kitchen can perfectly complement the rest of your home's look and feel.

What better space to refresh than where you freshen up! With anything and everything from replacing that noisy contractor-grade fan to replacing your tub surround with a custom designed tiled shower, we'll help you create your very own oasis.

If you're looking for greater functionality in your laundry or mudroom, we're ready to rise to the challenge. We can design and custom build drop zones great for neatly stowing shoes, backpacks, and jackets, or add cabinets for even more storage and organization.

The fireplace wall is one of the most underrated spaces to redesign and update. It's a focal point yet so often overlooked. Let us help you design and finish that space with mediums such as tile, stone, or custom wood trim and mantel. In addition, we can help you spice up any wall with features like shiplap, barnwood, board and batten, and more.

If a major renovation project is more than you want or need to undertake, consider a minor refresher! Pick and choose from lighting, countertops, flooring, paint, trim, or any other aspect of our customized offerings. It doesn't take tearing everything out and starting over to update and beautify a room! While no project is too small, don't be surprised if you find yourself adding to the list– as we share ideas you'll want to begin exploring your home's potential for greater beauty and functionality!
